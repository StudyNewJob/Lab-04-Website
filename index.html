But Why Star Wars?

Star Wars actually holds a special place in my heart and my entire family. My grand-mother and step grand-father had their first date watching the premiers of episode 6, A New Hope in 1977. I've read many of the Star Wars books and obviously have seen all the movies. It's my favorite IP!

Movies

Episode IV: A New Hope

Luke Skywalker joins forces with a Jedi Knight, a cocky pilot, a Wookiee, and two droids to save the galaxy from the Empire's world-destroying battle station, while also attempting to rescue Princess Leia from the mysterious Darth Vader.

Characters

Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker, a Force-sensitive human male, was a legendary Jedi Master who fought in the Galactic Civil War during the reign of the Galactic Empire. Along with his companions, Princess Leia Organa and Captain Han Solo, Skywalker served on the side of the Alliance to Restore the Republic.

Leia Organa

Leia Organa, a Force-sensitive human female, was one of the greatest leaders of the Rebel Alliance, fighting against the tyranny of the Galactic Empire.

Han Solo

Han Solo, a human male, was a smuggler from the manufacturing planet Corellia who achieved galactic fame as a member of the Rebel Alliance and later the New Republic.

Planets

Hoth

Hoth is the sixth planet in the remote system of the same name, and was the site of the Rebel Alliance's Echo Base. It is a world of snow and ice, surrounded by numerous moons, and home to deadly creatures like the wampa.

Coruscant

Coruscant, also known as the Imperial Center during the Imperial Era, is the political hub of the galaxy. Towering skyscrapers and futuristic technology make it a significant and influential planet.

Tatooine

Tatooine is a desert planet in a binary star system, and the first of three planets orbiting the suns. It is characterized by its arid climate and iconic sand dunes. It is the homeworld of Anakin and Luke Skywalker, who would go on to shape galactic history.

Starships

Millennium Falcon

The Millennium Falcon is a YT-1300 light freighter of the YT-1300f variety. Its aged appearance belies numerous advanced modifications to boost the ship's speed, weaponry, and shield, including a hyperdrive capable of traveling through hyperspace.

X-Wing Starfighter

The X-Wing starfighter is a versatile Rebel Alliance craft that balances speed with firepower, equipped with four laser cannons and two proton torpedo launchers, capable of going head-to-head with the Empire's best fighters.

Imperial Star Destroyer

The Imperial-class Star Destroyer, a symbol of the Empire's military might, has greater firepower than a full fleet of ordinary battleships. It can engage multiple enemy craft simultaneously, deploying TIE fighters for both attack and defense.
